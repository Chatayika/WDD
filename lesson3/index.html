City of Mutare Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this site is to inform visitors about City of Mutare Chamber of Commerce website , its purpose, target audience and scenarios. This site will describe the organization and structure and /style.

Site Name

The site will be hosted on https://mutarecity.com. It will be a source of information for all things regarding Mutare City.

Purpose

The main purpose of Mutare city Chamber of Commerce website is to promote investment and tourism within Mutare City thereby increasing the city's revenue. This goal will be met by the descriptions of opportunities for investment and tourism the city has to offer. Existing businesses and tourisist attractions will also be showcased to illustrate these opportunities. Through the website we will disseminate information concerning services, activities and events within the city. We would like visitors to our website to get a clear understanding of the services the city offers to both residents and non-residents. This includes the various resorts,tourist hot spots and recreational activities within the city. Our secondary objective is to deliver news and updates on events and activities happening within the city. This includes informing rate payers about our services , costs and city events and meetings. Through our website we will also receive suggestions from residents and rate payers. The website will serve as a bulletin to inform residents of issues that concern them in the city. Mutare City Chamber of Commerce website will serve a marketing and informing role.
