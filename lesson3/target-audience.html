Target audience

Our target audience are residents ad non-residents who would like to invest in our city. This means people across all age groups, gender and ethnicity with potential to travel. Our main focus is business people and income earners who would like to start businesses in our city as well as tourists who are looking for a spectacular and divine experience in our resorts. We are targeting a diverse range of tourists from individuals, groups, organisations to mountain hikers,dare devils and vibrant youths to families who would like to enjoy a quiet holiday.
