City of Mutare Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this site is to inform visitors about City of Mutare Chamber of Commerce website , its purpose, target audience and scenarios. This site will describe the organization and structure and /style.

Site Name

The site is going to be called City of Mutare Chamber of Commerce. It will be a source of information for all things regarding Mutare City.

Purpose

The main purpose of Mutare city Chamber of Commerce website is to promote investment and tourism within Mutare City thereby increasing the city's revenue. This goal will be met by the descriptions of opportunities for investment and tourism the city has to offer. Existing businesses and tourisist attractions will also be showcased to illustrate these opportunities. Through the website we will disseminate information concerning services, activities and events within the city. We would like visitors to our website to get a clear understanding of the services the city offers to both residents and non-residents. This includes the various resorts,tourist hot spots and recreational activities within the city. Our secondary objective is to deliver news and updates on events and activities happening within the city. This includes informing rate payers about our services , costs and city events and meetings. Through our website we will also receive suggestions form residents and rate payers. The website will serve as a bulletin to inform residents of issues that conern them in the city. Mutare City Chamber of Commerce website will serve a marketing and informing role.

Target audience

Our target audience are residents ad non-residents who would like to invest in our city. This means people across all age groups, gender and ethnicity with potential to travel. Our main focus is business people and income earners who would like to start businesses in our city as well as tourists who are looking for a spectacular and divine experience in our resorts. We are targeting a diverse range of tourists from individuals, groups, organisations to mountain hikers,dare devils and vibrant youths to families who would like to enjoy a quiet holiday.
