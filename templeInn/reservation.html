    Packages
    Accommodation Comments


    Basic package

    All rooms and suites supply basic in room comforts such as pillow mene iron and ironing Board, in-room safe, separate hanging closet,in-room heating and A/C controls, black-out shades, private bathroom with basic amenities including, hair dryer, bathtub. There is a separate vanity area and the kitchen comes with mini refrigerator,roobois tea and tea maker, coomplimentary bottled water. For entertainment we have various church magazines and books. A flat screen television which plays a selection of church movies, general conference talks and Tabernacle choir music videos. For our youth we have an ipod docking station and music from all church choirs including BYU. A work desk and lamp as well as a two line phone is available in each room

    Suites

    All suites have additional amenities such as a separate living room with a dining table, sofas. Bathrobes and slippers are provided in the bathrooms which have a separate/walk in shower. All suites are themed and decorated with art and history of the respective books in the book of Mormon

    Celestial Suite

    Celestial Suites are spacious apartments, villas and chalets with 3 bedroms, connecting living space and all basic amenities. They are suitable for large families
